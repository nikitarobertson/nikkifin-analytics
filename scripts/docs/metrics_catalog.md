### North Star

#### Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)

**Definition**

Total monthly subscription and add-on revenue from active subscribers.

**Formula**

```text
Subscription Revenue + Add-On Revenue
```

**Owner**

Executive Team

---

### Growth

#### Active Subscribers

**Definition**

Unique subscribers generating revenue during the month.

---

#### New Subscribers

**Definition**

Subscribers whose first paid month occurred during the selected month.

---

#### Activation Rate

**Definition**

Percent of trial users who became active paid subscribers.

**Formula**

```text
Activated Users / Trial Users
```

---

### Retention

#### Retention Rate

**Definition**

Percent of active subscribers retained from the previous month.

**Formula**

```text
Retained Subscribers / Prior Month Active Subscribers
```

---

#### Churn Rate

**Definition**

Percent of subscribers lost during the month.

**Formula**

```text
Churned Subscribers / Prior Month Active Subscribers
```

---

### Engagement

#### Monthly Active Users (MAU)

**Definition**

Subscribers with at least one session during the month.

---

#### Sessions Per User

**Definition**

Average monthly sessions per active subscriber.

---

#### Feature Adoption Rate

**Definition**

Percent of subscribers using at least one premium feature.

---

### Utilization

#### Tax Planning Utilization

#### Credit Monitoring Utilization

### Financial

#### Revenue Per Subscriber

**Definition**

Average monthly revenue generated per active subscriber.

---

#### Add-On Revenue %

**Definition**

Percent of total MRR generated by add-ons.

---

#### Premium Mix %

**Definition**

Percent of active subscribers on Premium tier.

---

### Planning

#### Forecast Variance

**Definition**

Difference between forecasted and actual MRR.

---

#### Budget Variance

**Definition**

Difference between budgeted and actual MRR.

**Formula**

```text
Actual MRR - Budget MRR
```
