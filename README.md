## NikkiFin Analytics Platform

NovaFin Analytics Platform is a synthetic analytics ecosystem designed to demonstrate how modern analytics teams drive executive decision-making through metric design, forecasting, self-service reporting and AI-powered workflows.

This project recreates the types of analytics products I have built throughout my career using synthetic data and publicly shareable code.

---

### Important Note on Data & Company Design

Everything within this portfolio has been intentionally created from scratch.

NovaFin is a fictional company developed specifically for this project and does not represent any real business, employer, client, customer, or dataset.

All datasets, metrics, reporting structures, forecasts, business scenarios, customer records, financial results, and operational processes have been synthetically generated for educational and portfolio purposes.

No proprietary, confidential, regulated, or employer-owned information has been used in the creation of this portfolio.

The purpose of NovaFin is to demonstrate analytics strategy, analytics engineering, business intelligence, forecasting, metric design, and leadership capabilities within a fully transparent and reproducible environment.

By creating both the business and the underlying data model from the ground up, this project showcases not only technical implementation skills but also the ability to think like a business stakeholder when designing analytics systems.

### Goal

Analytics should do more than produce dashboards.

The goal of this portfolio is demonstrate how analytics can:

- Define and govern company metrics
- Create executive visibility into business performance
- Enable self-service reporting
- Support strategic planning and forecasting
- Drive operational efficiency
- Scale through automation and AI

---

### Fictional Company

NovaFin is a subscription-based fintech company focused on helping individuals manage and grow their financial wellbeing.

The company acquires customers through multiple channels, offers subscription-based products, and tracks growth through a company-wide North Star metric framework.
